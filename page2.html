Top 5 Most Influential Jazz Artists

  1. Louis Armstrong – The father of modern jazz trumpet
  2. Miles Davis – Innovator of multiple jazz styles
  3. John Coltrane – Master of saxophone and spiritual jazz
  4. Duke Ellington – Legendary composer and bandleader
  5. Ella Fitzgerald – The First Lady of Song

Miles Davis: The Cool Innovator

Miles Dewey Davis III (1926–1991) revolutionized jazz multiple times throughout his career, from bebop to cool jazz to fusion.

His albums "Kind of Blue" and "Bitches Brew" changed the course of music history.

Jazz Evolution Timeline

  • 1920s: New Orleans/Dixieland Jazz
  • 1930s–40s: Swing Era and Big Bands
  • 1940s: Bebop Revolution
  • 1950s: Cool Jazz and Hard Bop
  • 1960s: Modal and Free Jazz
  • 1970s+: Jazz Fusion and Modern Styles
